# Hostel Management System Report

## Introduction
The Hostel Management System aims to streamline hostel administration tasks, enhance communication between stakeholders, and improve the overall hostel experience for students, staff, and administrators at XYZ College.

## Functionalities
1. **User Management**
   - Registration: Students, Staff, Administrators
   - Login: Authentication and Authorization
   - Password Management: Reset, Change

2. **Student Management**
   - Add/Remove Students
   - Student Profiles: Personal Information, Contact Details, Room Allotment
   - Room Allocation: Assigning students to rooms
   - Room Transfer: Transfer students between rooms
   - Attendance Tracking

3. **Room Management**
   - Room Types: Single, Double, Shared
   - Room Allocation: Allocation of rooms to students
   - Room Facilities: Amenities, Furniture details
   - Room Maintenance: Record maintenance activities

4. **Staff Management**
   - Add/Remove Staff Members
   - Staff Profiles: Personal Information, Contact Details, Role

5. **Inventory Management**
   - Manage Hostel Supplies: Food, Cleaning Supplies, etc.
   - Inventory Tracking: Stock levels, Purchases, Consumption

6. **Attendance Management**
   - Student Attendance: Track daily attendance
   - Leave Management: Apply for leave, Approval

7. **Complaint Management**
   - Register Complaints: Students, Staff
   - Resolution: Assign, Track, Resolve complaints

8. **Billing and Payments**
   - Fee Management: Student Dues, Payment Tracking
   - Expense Management: Utilities, Maintenance
   - Billing Reports: Generate Bills, Payment Records

9. **Notice Board**
   - Important Announcements: Notices, Events, Holidays
